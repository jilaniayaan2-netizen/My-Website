I love aliens and 100% I believe they exist, though they likely haven't been to our planet just yet. My love for aliens comes from the various speculative biology videos projects I've seen, if you don't know what speculative biology, its the concept of theorizing how something could possibly evolve. I've been so inspired by this that the story I'm writing started out as an excuse to make an alien ecosystem.

The Speculative World of C-100

C-100 is the planet that my story, Expedition to C-100, takes place on. Before I show off all the amazing drawings my brother has made of the aliens along with their biology, I want to write about how details of the planet.

Planet attributes

Firstly, C-100 is a planet that's slighty smaller than earth meaning its gravity is significantly weaker than earth's. THe atmosphere is slighty denser and there is a higher oxygen level, not a deadly amount but it is comparable to the oxygen level during the Carbiniforous period. The planet is split into two mega continents, one in the east and one in the west. The western continent is mostly dense rainforest, except for the south, where it is mostly grasslands, and the north, where its mostly mountains. A large interconnected cave system lies underneath the western continent which holds its own ecosystem. The western continent is split into two by the western interior seaway and there are various islands in this seaway and around the continent. Around the seaway there is a swamp biome, to the north you enter a rocky and volcanic area, the middle of the continent is mostly dry grassland and small forests. The western continent lacks rainforests due to the fact its lower down south, which makes it too cold for most of the large tree-like organisms.

The Limb Debacle

The limb debacle is about the number of limbs for my aliens, which was one of the hardest things to figure out because I obviously did not want to have 4 limbs(that'd be boring and basic) but how would something that has 6 limbs or 2 limbs evolve? Because here on earth almost all vertabrates have 4 limbs because the first fish to crawl onto land also happened to have 4 limbs. So, by that logic, I made it that on C-100, 3 different species came onto land around the same time and evolved into various different land vertabrates. Each of the aliens had a different number of limbs, those being: 6, 4, and 2. So all vertabrates on C-100 will have 6, 4, or 2 limbs. This also means that if two aliens have the same amount of limbs that they are distantly related. Anyways, I can let you through now that I've cleared up the whole limb thing.

Volitare Silva

Volitare Silva is one of the few creatures that lives in the sky that has 4 limbs, this is due to the strange story of it's evolution which is very similar to that of earth whales. It likely started as a small predator living in the mountains but began to slowly evolve to get food from the air over millions of years. Volitare Silva do not fly, instead they act more like a ballon, having a large organ on it's head that produces and stores helium, which is more than enough to keep it afloat. Volitare Silva are also incredibly dumb with tiny brains. They do stick together in groups but often end up floating away, carried by the wind currents. The most interesting thing about this alien is it's various symbiotic relationships that make it a keystone species. Firstly, many species of plants spread their seeds into the air, hoping to get on the backs of Volitare Silvas to avoid predation. This in turn camouflages it and also attracts Fluitanis Draconis hives to live on the Volitare Silva, which in turn helps protect the defensless creature from predation.

Spinosa Lingua Venator

Spinosa L. Venator is a giant and specialized ariel predator. It has a beak that splits open in 3 different directions, which it uses to grip the slippery skin of Volitare Silva before it uses its harpoon-like tongue to pierce through it's hide and pop the organ that stores the helium. Spinosa L. Ventors are a completely air born species, they do not land for any reason and spend their whole lives in the sky. They give live birth and the second the baby is born, they are able to fly. Their wings act similarly to oars on a large ship, propelling through the air. They also happen to mate for life, forming deep social bonds with their partners. Males during mating season turn a deep purple and use the small spike at the end of their tail to dance for the females, the females will decide their mate based on their color, tail spike, and how well they can dance. The dance consists of doing loops and sharp turns in the air while also whipping around their tail.

Cristatus Poncu Cursor

Cristatus Poncu Cursor is a large herbivourous species that lives exclusively in the Poncu Savannah in the middle of the western continent. They live in large herds similar to water buffalo, similarly to water buffalo, they are incredibly short tempered and are quick to mob violence. There are multiple instances of an angry herd trampelling and killing lesser predators. They evolved to have multiple pairs of eyes to give themselves a near 360 degree field of vision, an adaptation to avoid the larger and more powerful predators. This species also shows great sexual dimorphism with the males being a iridescent dark green along with having a huge frill, while the females are a light reddish-brown which allows them to blend into the red plants of the savannah. Males use their frills to compete with one another for mates and for the right to lead the herd. These confrontations aren't physical, instead, the two males sing and flare open their frills while circling one another. During this, the male with the biggest and most colorful frill along with the best singing(which is similar to that of a song birds) wins.

Armato Rege

Armato Rege is the apex predator of the western continent, with its range being the entire continent. Its front limbs are double jointed which allows it to use them for running or for slamming and grabbing lesser aliens. This on top of it being heavily armored makes it impossible for prey to fight back. So, how did it evolve armor considering that its a predator? This is because it shares a lot of its habitat with Terram Cete, a blue whale sized subterranean ambush predator, so to prevent being mauled by these giants, Armato Rege evolved armored spikes. If you go down to the north or forest, out of the range of Terram Cete, you'll notice the Armato Rege there are smaller and have weaker armor. Armato Rege are very intelligent, being curious about their surrondings and having a strong maternal instinct. Young Reges are raised up until the age of 10, which by then, they are capable of hunting on their own. These younglings then will join up into a group of 4-5 to hunt together called a "gang". These gangs will bully lesser creatures but occasionally will also try their hand at hunting Terram Cetes. They do this by using one individual to lure it out before the others grab it's limbs to pin it down while the last one goes for it's throat. Now, often they only do this when desperate and these hunts often end in 1 or more gang members being killed.

Poncu Foidens

Poncu Foidens is acctually distnantly related to Cristatus Poncu Cursor but instead of going the route of giant social herds, they've instead opted for a more rodent like lifestyle, feeding mostly on grasses and roots. They burrow underground and live in small families of 5 individuals. These small creatures mate for life. Single males are known as den masters and will gaurd their burrow from other males, which often leads to fights. Once the male is able to attract a female, using a string of complex songs and dances which use their frills and antennae.

Volantem Interfectorem

Volantem Interfectorem is a medium sized ariel predator that has a hunting strategy similar to a hawk, they dive up high before doing a low, high speed sweep through the tall grass to snap up any small creatures with their sideways jawz. They have eyes similar to that of a fly, which combined with their extra pair of wings makes them excellent at maneuvering through the air and avoiding larger predators in the air. Unlike other air predators, they are not permenetly airborn as their wings can fold, allowing them to walk similarly to large pterosaurs. They nest at the top of the large tree-like organisms, using the flat discs to build nests where they lay eggs and rear their young.

Terram Cete

Terram Cete, as mentioned previously in the Armato Rege entry, is a massive super predator which gets up to 82 feet long. During their youth, they build up giant burrows which is where they live once they get larger. Once they get to their greatest size, they cannot move around the earth and tend to stay in their burrow. These super predators only need to rarely eat as their metabolism is so slow that it can go years without eating but when they do eat, they eat a massive amount of food. These creatures can reproduce asexually through parthenogenesis, this was evolved over hundreds of years as the Terram Cete got bigger and bigger which made it harder and harder to find mates. Terram Cete are completely blind and also have only two limbs, which is something rare for land animals on C-100.

Luminare Reptans

Luminare Reptans are small, dog sized creatures that hunt in packs. Similarly to Terram Cete, they are blind and have two limbs. They are covered in head to toe with osteoderms and thick, leathery skin. These creatures are incredibly intelligent, having their own complex language that they use communicate with one another during hunts. Their tails are prehensile and they acctaully have opposible thumbs, some packs have been observed using tools such as primative spears. Other's were even observed carving wood into complex shapes like other aliens.

Invisibilia Interfectorem

Invisibilia Interfectorem is a very specialized predator that lives in a small region where instead of forests made up of towering tree-like plants, they are made of purplish bamboo-like plants. It's eyes are on the underside of its head so that it can scan the forest floor for prey and its limbs are similar to skewers and are used to impale soft bodied prey. If this alien's legs are damaged, then it is most likely doomed to starve.

Ferrum Maxilla

Ferrum Maxilla is a small herbivourous creature that lives in the bamboo forests that the Invisibilia Interfectorem lives in as well. Electrica Interfectorem

Electrica Interfectorem is a 4 limbed aqautic predator that lives in the rivers and lakes of the western continent. It's entire body is covered in holes, which produce fleshy orbs.
